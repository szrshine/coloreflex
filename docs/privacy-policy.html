Privacy Policy for coloreflex

Last Updated: November 11, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to coloreflex. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile game application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

When you use coloreflex, we may automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • Unique device identifiers
  • Game play data (scores, achievements, game statistics)
  • Usage data (app open/close times, session duration)
  • Crash logs and error reports

Information You Provide

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Create a user profile or nickname
  • Participate in leaderboards
  • Contact our support team
  • Provide feedback or reviews

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the game
  • Improve and personalize your gaming experience
  • Track game statistics and achievements
  • Display and manage leaderboards
  • Analyze usage patterns and optimize game performance
  • Debug and fix technical issues
  • Send you updates and notifications (with your consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Storage

Your game data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. Some data may be synced to cloud services for:

  • Leaderboard functionality
  • Cross-device game progress (future feature)
  • Backup and restore capabilities

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Firebase: For analytics, crash reporting, and cloud services
  • Sentry: For error tracking and crash reporting
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements (future feature)

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With service providers who assist in operating our game

Children's Privacy

coloreflex is designed to be family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy. Local data on your device can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
